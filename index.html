For Rani

A message of strength and love.

Dear Rani, on a day like this, we know the world can feel a little quieter, a little heavier. It’s a day filled with memories, with unspoken words, and with the profound ache of missing someone so deeply. Your father was a hero in the truest sense, a guardian who stood watch over our nation at its border. His sacrifice is a story of immense bravery, a legacy that now lives on brightly and fiercely within you.

Though you never had the chance for a final goodbye, a love like his doesn't end. It transforms. It becomes the quiet strength you feel in moments of doubt, the gentle warmth of the sun on your face, the guiding star in the darkest night. He is with you always, a silent protector for you, your sister, and your mother—forever wrapped in the honor of the flag he served.

His greatest wish would be simple: to see his girls live with joy, with courage, and with the same strength he showed the world. He watches over you with a father's endless pride, celebrating the incredible young woman you are becoming. The bond you share with your mother and sister is the most beautiful tribute to his memory. Know that you are never alone in this. We all stand with you, today and always. Face the future with your head held high, and feel his love with every step you take.

In Loving Memory
